Im Happy You Found This Amazing Page On A Great Guy By The Name Of Ralph Lauren

American fashion designer Ralph Lauren is a household name, associated with classic elegance and classic American style. Ralph Lifshitz, who was born in the Bronx, New York, in 1939, Lauren's rise from deprived origins to international fashion tycoon is truly amazing. When he established his own company in 1967, he first sold a range of men's ties that were meant to exude an air of classy sophistication.
